January 19, 2026

Meeting with the Minister of Higher Education

ENP Racing Team had the honor of presenting our Formula Student project to the Minister of Higher Education at the ENP Incubator. This milestone meeting showcased our team's dedication and innovation in building Algeria's first combustion Formula Student car.

The Minister expressed strong support for our initiative and emphasized the importance of such projects in developing Algeria's engineering capabilities and fostering innovation among students.
